# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 21, 2026

## Overview

Content Factory is a personal automation tool for uploading videos to social media platforms.

## Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app accesses your TikTok account solely to upload video content on your behalf. No account information, analytics, or user data is retained after the upload is complete.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the TikTok Content Posting API. Your use of TikTok is subject to [TikTok's own Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/us/privacy-policy/en).
